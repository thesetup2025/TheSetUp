Daylight

While the sun lasts.

If Afterglow is the night, Daylight is the day. Outdoor experiences that make the most of Sotogrande's light, water and space. Friendly competition, nature and celebration in a single afternoon.

01
Sport + food

Padel or tennis tournament + asado

A match or mini-tournament on the best courts in the area, followed by a proper outdoor asado. The right way to burn and celebrate in the same afternoon.

02
Equestrian sport

Polo afternoon

Private access to some of Europe's finest polo fields, with bespoke hospitality and curated catering. Sotogrande is one of the sport's great homes — you should experience it properly.

03
On the water

Boat trip / private regatta

Sailing from Sotogrande harbour towards the Strait or the Andalusian coast, with crew, catering and sunset at sea. Or a proper private regatta if competition is more your thing.
